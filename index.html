Our Story

DEVINE DINE was born from a passion for exceptional cuisine and the rich culinary heritage of South Africa. Founded in 2010 by award-winning chef Nkateko Nobela, our restaurant brings a modern twist to traditional South African flavors.

Located in the heart of Cape Town, DEVINE DINE offers an intimate dining experience where every dish tells a story. Our menu celebrates the diversity of South African ingredients, sourced locally from trusted farmers and producers.

Our elegant atmosphere, impeccable service, and innovative cuisine have earned us recognition as one of South Africa's premier fine dining destinations.
